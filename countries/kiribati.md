**19 March 2020**
<br />The Government of Australia has announced that from 9pm local time on 20 March, entry to the country will only be permitted to Australian citizens or permanent residents. This follows a similar announcement by New Zealand from 19 March. As many flight connections to and from Kiribati connect in Australia and New Zealand, options to depart Kiribati are likely to reduce. If you’re currently a visitor in Kiribati you’re advised to prepare to leave as soon as possible. If you’re resident in Kiribati, you should be aware of the potential impact of the border closures and flight restrictions on future travel plans.
