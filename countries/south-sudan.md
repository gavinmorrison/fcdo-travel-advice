# South Sudan

> **[View current FCDO travel advice for South Sudan](https://www.gov.uk/foreign-travel-advice/south-sudan)**

## Current Status: 🔴

FCDO advises against all travel to South Sudan.

*Last reviewed by FCDO: 10 December 2025*
