# Ghana

> **[View current FCDO travel advice for Ghana](https://www.gov.uk/foreign-travel-advice/ghana)**

## Current Status: 🟡

FCDO advises against all but essential travel to parts of Ghana.

*Last reviewed by FCDO: 10 December 2025*

**20 March 2020**
