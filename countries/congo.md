# Congo

> **[View current FCDO travel advice for Congo](https://www.gov.uk/foreign-travel-advice/congo)**

## Current Status: ⚠️

FCDO advises against all travel to parts of Congo.

*Last reviewed by FCDO: 10 December 2025*

## Change History

### 2025

**10 December 2025**
New information about dual nationals returning to the UK (‘Entry requirements’ page).

**20 January 2025**
FCDO no longer advises against all but essential travel to some districts in Pool Region and Mouyondzi District in the Bouenza Region ('Warnings and insurance' page).
