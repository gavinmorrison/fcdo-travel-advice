**15 March 2020**
The Foreign and Commonwealth Office advise against all but essential travel to Timor-Leste (East Timor). There is an ongoing global outbreak of coronavirus (COVID-19). Detection and treatment of the disease in Timor-Leste will be difficult, given the limited healthcare available. There is a risk that the healthcare system will come under significant strain as COVID-19 spreads.  Medical evacuation may not be available or be very expensive. (Summary and Health)
